Barbacoa flautas

Servings: Make 3 per person
Prep time: 10
Cook time: 20

20%
85%

Ingredients

Item Quatity
Shredded barbacoa (from Instant Pot Barbacoa recipe) As much as you have leftover
Fajita-size flour tortillas As many as you need
Shredded Chihuahua cheese (or Monterey Jack) As much as you need

Instructions

  1. Warm tortillas in microwave for 30-60 seconds until warm and pliable
  2. Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Line baking sheet with parchment and spray with nonstick cooking spray
  3. Assemble flautas with a couple tablespoons of meat and a tablespoon or so of cheese
  4. Roll tight like a cigar and place seam side down on prepared baking sheet
  5. Spray the tops of the flautas with nonstick cooking spray
  6. Bake for 20 minutes, turning over after 10

Notes

Serve with taco sauce!

Ortega is good, but our personal favorite is a bottle of Taco Bell fire sauce.

Reheating

Once the flautas have cooled you can place them in zip top freezer bag. When ready to reheat, place frozen flauta in microwave and heat in 30 second increments. Usually are warmed through in 60-90 seconds.
